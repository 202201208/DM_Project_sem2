Introduction

Digital image processing, also known as DIP, is a specialized area within computer science focused on utilizing digital computers to manipulate digital images. It involves various techniques and algorithms to enhance, modify, or extract information from digital images.

Digital image processing is a powerful tool that can be used to improve the quality, analyze, and understand images. It is a rapidly growing field with many new applications being developed all the time. Digital image processing is widely used in many fields, including medicine, astronomy, remote sensing, robotics, surveillance, and entertainment.
